The Glass Box methodology

We don't build black boxes.
Everything is auditable.

Mid-market enterprises in logistics, legal, and FinTech cannot route operational data through SaaS tools they don't control. We build private pipelines that run inside your infrastructure — fully auditable, with zero vendor dependency.

✗  Standard SaaS AI tools
Your data processed on the vendor's servers — you don't know where it goes
No audit trail — you can't see what decisions were made or why
System stops working the moment you stop paying
Third-party API access to your financial, client, or operational data
Vendor lock-in — impossible to migrate without losing everything
✓  Glass Box — hytGenX
Every pipeline runs inside your infrastructure — AWS, Azure, VPC, or on-premise
Every data flow, model call, and decision is logged and auditable by your team
You own the code — if we stop working together, your systems keep running
No third-party vendor has ongoing access to your operational data
Documented, maintainable, extensible by your internal team
🔒
Private by architecture
We deploy directly into your infrastructure. No SaaS middleware. No third-party processing. Your data stays in your environment from ingestion to output.
🔍
Auditable by design
Every pipeline includes comprehensive logging. You can trace exactly what was processed, when, by which model, and what decision was made. Required for compliance.
📦
Owned, not licensed
The code belongs to you. Documented and maintainable. No subscription to hytGenX required for your systems to keep running.
