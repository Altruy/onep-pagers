We designed it this way because we only want to work with clients who find genuine value at every step. Lock-in produces bad work. Each stage should earn the next one.

Is this right for you?

We work with a specific kind of client.

This works well if
You spend more than eight hours a week on work that should already be automated — and you know it
You have tried a point solution (a Zapier flow, a tool subscription) and it either broke, did not scale, or did not go far enough
You have a clear bottleneck in your operations and no confident answer on what to do about it
You need to demonstrate a result — to a board, an investor, or yourself — within a defined timeframe
You want a system that runs in production, not a proof of concept that needs babysitting
This is not the right fit if
You want someone to install a tool and hand it over — we build systems engineered around your specific operations
You need a generic AI chatbot or a pre-built workflow applied without customisation
You are not willing to invest two to three hours of your own time in the discovery and strategy phases
You are looking for the lowest-cost option — our work is priced against the value it creates, not against off-the-shelf rates
Common questions

Before you book.

What actually happens in the free session?
A 20-minute video call. I ask you questions about your operations, your team, and where you feel the most friction. At the end, I tell you honestly whether AI automation is the right investment for you right now, and where I would start if it is. There is no pitch at the end of the call.
What if I do the strategy engagement and decide not to proceed?
You keep the strategy document. It describes exactly what we found and what we would build. You can use it to brief another consultant, build it internally, or revisit it when the timing is right. We do not put restrictions on it.
How long does a full engagement take?
Discovery is 20 minutes. The strategy document takes one to two weeks depending on process complexity. Implementation timelines vary by scope — most projects run between four and twelve weeks from kickoff to handover.
Do I need a technical team on my side?
No. We handle the full build, integration, and handover. Your involvement is to describe how your operations work and to confirm that what we deliver fits your workflow. Technical knowledge is not required.
What does it cost?
The discovery session is free. Strategy and planning are fixed-fee engagements — you receive a clear number before each stage begins. Build work is scoped and quoted per project. You will always know the cost before any work starts.
Why only four active engagements at once?
Because every engagement gets my direct attention, not a team of contractors. The cap exists to protect the quality of the work. If spots are full, you can join the waitlist and I will reach out when there is availability.
