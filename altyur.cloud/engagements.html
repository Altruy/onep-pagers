Commercial model

How we engage —
project structure and commercial model.

Three structured engagement models. Each is designed for a specific stage of your AI operations journey. You can enter at any tier depending on where you are and what your organisation needs to move forward.

Tier 1
AI Operations Blueprint
Fixed scope, fixed fee. Typically 1–2 weeks. The deliverable is a document your board can approve.
Moves to: Deployment Engagement if the Blueprint surfaces clear build priorities.

What it is

A 5–7 day structured audit of your operations. We map your processes, identify the top 3–5 automation opportunities, model the ROI, and deliver a prioritised roadmap with tooling recommendations and implementation timelines.

What you get

  • Process documentation for the workflows we audit
  • Ranked automation opportunity list with effort and impact scoring
  • ROI model for the top opportunities
  • Phased implementation roadmap with tooling recommendations
  • Executive summary formatted for leadership approval

Who it is for

Organisations that want a clear picture before committing to a build. The Blueprint is useful whether you build with us or not — you can take it to any contractor. It is independent of any specific tooling vendor or platform.

Tier 2
Deployment Engagement
Scoped and quoted per project. Most deployments deliver positive ROI within the first 90 days of operation.
Moves to: Ongoing Operations Retainer if system management or expansion is required.

What it is

We build exactly what the Blueprint identified. Private pipelines deployed into your infrastructure. Full documentation, QA, testing, and structured handover — so your team owns and understands what was built.

What you get

  • Production AI systems running inside your environment
  • Full code ownership — no platform lock-in
  • Technical documentation and operational runbooks
  • Monitoring setup and exception routing configuration
  • 30-day support window post-launch

Who it is for

Organisations with a defined automation opportunity and leadership approval to build. Typical engagements cover one or two major workflow automations. The scope is fixed before any build begins — there are no open-ended retainers at this stage.

Tier 3
Ongoing Operations Retainer
Monthly retainer, minimum 3-month commitment. Scales with the scope of active systems.

What it is

We stay on as your AI operations partner. Monthly retainer covers system monitoring, iteration, expansion of existing pipelines, and new capability development as your operations evolve. You do not need to hire an internal AI team — we operate as one.

What you get

  • A dedicated point of contact — our CTO, directly
  • Monthly reporting on system performance and operational metrics
  • Priority access to new capabilities as they become production-ready
  • Continuous pipeline improvement and iteration
  • New automation scope added without a new project cycle

Who it is for

Organisations with live AI operations that want ongoing development without building an internal AI team. Retainer clients typically have two or more active production systems and a clear roadmap of further automation opportunities already identified.

Questions

Common questions about engaging hytGenX.

Do we need to complete all three tiers?
No. Some organisations bring us in at the Deployment stage with their own internal discovery already done. The Blueprint exists for those who want external validation before committing. If you have a clearly scoped build and leadership sign-off, we can start there.
Who owns the code and systems after deployment?
You do. Entirely. Our deployments produce documented, maintainable code that your team can operate and extend. No subscription to hytGenX is required for the systems to keep running. We build for handover from the first day of the engagement.
What does "private pipeline" mean in practice?
It means the AI systems run on infrastructure you control — your AWS account, your Azure environment, or your on-premise servers. hytGenX has no ongoing access to your operational data. Everything is configured to run inside your environment before we hand over. We build systems that run inside your environment. Not ours.
How do we start if we are not sure which tier fits?
Start with a free 20-minute conversation. We will tell you honestly where you sit and what engagement structure makes sense for your situation. No sales process, no pitch deck. Just a direct conversation about where you are and what actually makes sense.
