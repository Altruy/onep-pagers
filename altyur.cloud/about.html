Background

My background spans multi-agent systems, RevOps intelligence, voice AI, workflow automation, and LLM infrastructure. I have delivered over 50 production AI systems across marketing, real estate, healthcare, finance, education, and SaaS.

I am the CTO of hytGenX.ai, a US-registered AI systems engineering firm. My consulting practice — altyur.cloud — is where I take on direct client engagements. The scope is deliberate: I work with a small number of clients at a time, giving each engagement the same depth of attention I would give to an internal project.

Before focusing on AI systems, I spent years working across the full stack of modern business technology — CRMs, automation platforms, data pipelines, and analytics. That background is what makes the work practical. I know what breaks in production, what gets adopted by teams, and what sounds good in a pitch but rarely ships.

I have been recognised as an Expert Vetted freelancer on Upwork, placing in the top 1% of AI and automation professionals on the platform. Most of my new client relationships come through referral or direct outreach — the work tends to speak for itself.
