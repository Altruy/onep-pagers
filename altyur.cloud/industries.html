Glass Box AI by vertical

Your data does not leave
your environment.

Mid-market enterprises in data-sensitive verticals cannot afford to route operational intelligence through third-party SaaS. Glass Box means private pipelines that run inside your own infrastructure — with full auditability and zero vendor lock-in. Below is what that looks like for the four verticals we serve.

Logistics
Logistics & Supply Chain
Logistics operations generate some of the most competitively sensitive data in any business. Supplier pricing, route efficiency, carrier relationships, and demand forecasting models are all operational intelligence that a 3PL or freight operator cannot afford to have processed on servers outside their control. Glass Box pipelines keep that data inside your environment while still automating the workflows that consume the most analyst and ops time.
Freight invoice reconciliation and dispute automation — matching carrier invoices against rate cards and flagging discrepancies without manual review
Demand forecasting pipelines connected directly to ERP and WMS systems (SAP, Oracle, and others)
Supplier communication and purchase order automation triggered by inventory signals
Operations reporting and exception alerting for dispatch and warehouse teams on a defined schedule
Carrier performance monitoring with automatic SLA breach detection and escalation routing
See the executive reporting case study
Legal
Legal & Professional Services
Attorney-client privilege, bar ethics rules, and client confidentiality requirements make it categorically impossible to route legal work product through consumer AI tools. Law firms and professional services firms that want to automate internal operations need systems where they can demonstrate exactly where data went and prove it never left their environment. That is not a preference — in most jurisdictions it is an obligation.
Matter management and workflow automation — conflict checks, deadline tracking, and task routing without manual coordination
Time entry and billing automation generated from activity logs, eliminating manual time capture
Contract lifecycle management and document routing with version control
Client status update pipelines that auto-generate progress summaries from matter notes
Internal knowledge management — RAG pipelines on case law, precedent documents, and firm SOPs with source citations
See the invoice automation case study
FinTech
FinTech & Financial Services
SOC 2, PCI-DSS, and financial regulatory frameworks create compliance obligations that are directly implicated by where data is processed. FinTech firms and financial services companies face real regulatory exposure if operational data — transaction records, client financials, reconciliation outputs — is processed on third-party infrastructure they cannot audit. Glass Box architecture resolves that exposure at the infrastructure level, not the policy level.
Transaction reconciliation automation across payment systems and accounting platforms with a complete audit trail
Compliance reporting pipeline automation for AML, KYC, and regulatory filing workflows
Client reporting and portfolio performance narrative generation on a defined schedule
Commission and payout calculation engines with full auditability from deal close to disbursement
Internal ops automation — approval workflows, exception routing, and escalation pipelines that do not touch third-party servers
See the commission engine case study
B2B SaaS
B2B SaaS & Technology
B2B SaaS companies have a distinct sensitivity: operational data reflects growth trajectory, customer base composition, and pricing strategy — all of which are commercially valuable. PLG companies with usage signal data, sales teams with pipeline intelligence, and finance teams with ARR analytics all have reason to keep their operational intelligence inside their own environment rather than passing it through a SaaS vendor's AI layer.
RevOps intelligence via MCP — Claude queries live HubSpot, Stripe, and billing data in plain English, privately, with no data leaving your stack
Product usage signal monitoring with automated expansion opportunity and churn risk detection
Automated onboarding sequences triggered by in-app behaviour milestones
Commission engine connected to CRM deal data with real-time recalculation as deals close
Executive reporting and board pack automation on a fixed schedule with AI-generated commentary
See the RevOps case study
